MS Seamless Pipe

MS (Mild Steel) Seamless Pipes manufactured without welding, ensuring higher strength and uniform structure for high-pressure applications.

High tensile strength
Corrosion resistance
Leak-proof design

Applications:

Oil refineries, petrochemical plants, boilers, heat exchangers

MS ERW Pipe (Electric Resistance Welded)

Formed by rolling metal and welding longitudinally. ERW Pipes are economical and efficient for medium to low-pressure applications.

Smooth finish
Dimensionally accurate
Various thicknesses

Applications:

Structural support, engineering, water pipelines, scaffolding

Versatile

MS Square Pipe

Square pipes preferred for applications requiring strength, surface area, and aesthetics. Uniform structure for architectural usage.

Rust-resistant coating
Strong and lightweight
Easy to weld

Applications:

Building frameworks, furniture, fencing, support columns

MS Hydraulic Pipe

Specially designed to carry pressurized fluids in hydraulic machinery. High resistance to cracking and pressure fluctuation.

Superior pressure tolerance
Seamless finish
High-performance durability

Applications:

Heavy machinery, hydraulic lifts, brake systems

Structural

MS Angle

L-shaped structural steel used in construction and fabrication for support frameworks. Versatile material for various applications.

Uniform thickness
Corrosion-resistant
Strong load-bearing capacity

Applications:

Bridges, industrial sheds, transmission towers

Support

MS Channel

C-Channels or ISMC are roll-formed steel sections providing high structural support in frameworks and mechanical constructions.

High strength-to-weight ratio
Rigid structure
Customizable length

Applications:

Machine frames, trailers, building reinforcements

Fabrication

MS Plate

Flat sheets of mild steel used in wide range of applications due to machinability and flexibility. Suitable for welding and fabrication.

Easy to cut and weld
Customizable dimensions
Sturdy performance

Applications:

Base plates, fabrication, shipping containers, tanks

Multi-Purpose

MS Flat Bar

Rectangular sections that can be easily drilled, cut, and welded, making them ideal for fabrication and structural reinforcement.

High dimensional accuracy
Smooth surface
Long service life

Applications:

Gratings, brackets, base plates, support structures

Corrosion Resistant

GI Pipe (Galvanized Iron Pipe)

Zinc-coated pipes to prevent corrosion and extend life in moist or outdoor conditions. Ideal for plumbing and water supply systems.

Rust-resistant
Durable in all weather
Long life cycle

Applications:

Water pipelines, fencing, agricultural use, scaffolding

MS Beam

I-shaped steel sections used as main load-bearing components in construction. Vital for large-scale engineering projects.

Superior load distribution
Excellent strength
Multiple sizes available

Bridges, buildings, shipbuilding, railways
