Our Story

Established in 2010, Goyal Pipes and Tubes has emerged as a trusted name in the trading and supply of superior quality piping solutions. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we specialize in E.R.W. Seamless Pipes, Hydraulic Pipes, Boiler Tubes, M.S. & G.I. Pipes, and comprehensive structural solutions.

Our Mission

Our wide product portfolio is sourced from certified and reliable vendors, ensuring unmatched durability, high pressure-bearing capacity, corrosion resistance, and excellent finish. We understand the critical standards of each sector and offer customized solutions.

Leadership

Under the leadership of Mr. Rakesh Agrawal, we have built long-lasting relationships with clients and vendors by maintaining transparency, competitive pricing, and customer-first ethics. Our dedicated team ensures smooth operations and quality delivery.
